About This Project

Revive is an AI-powered photo restoration app that breathes new life into your old, damaged, or faded photographs. Using advanced machine learning algorithms, it can restore scratches, repair damage, enhance colors, and improve the overall quality of your cherished memories.

Key Features

  • Scratch and damage repair
