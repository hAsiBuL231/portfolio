About This Project

Aidfast is a comprehensive healthcare platform designed to bridge the gap between patients and healthcare providers in Bangladesh. The app streamlines the doctor appointment booking process with an intuitive interface and efficient scheduling system.

Built with modern technologies and a focus on user experience, Aidfast makes healthcare accessible to everyone with just a few taps.

Key Features

  • Easy doctor search and appointment booking
  • Real-time schedule availability
  • Digital prescription management
  • Patient history and records
  • Appointment reminders and notifications
