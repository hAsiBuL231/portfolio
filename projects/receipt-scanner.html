About This Project

Receipt Scanner Spend Tracker is a smart financial management app designed to simplify expense tracking. Using advanced OCR (Optical Character Recognition) technology, it automatically extracts information from your receipts and categorizes your spending.

Whether you're tracking personal expenses, managing a small business, or preparing for tax season, this app provides the tools you need to stay on top of your finances with minimal effort.

Key Features

  • AI-powered receipt scanning with automatic text extraction
  • Smart expense categorization
  • Detailed spending analytics and reports
  • Budget tracking and alerts
  • Export data for accounting purposes
  • Secure cloud backup
